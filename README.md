# 🐕 Pluto AI Assistant

Imagine having a friend who's always by your side, offering suggestions and guidance without feeling like a robot. This AI tool, PLUTO, can provide real-time advice in any situation, whether you're meeting someone or need instant suggestions. It's not your regular ChatGPT.

As time progresses, more options will become available for use! Introducing the two-box system: this is where the real magic happens. PLUTO always listens to what you're doing. For example, if you're talking to a friend, PLUTO can assist you in the conversation by listening and offering suggestions. If you encounter a question you can't answer, PLUTO can provide assistance without the need for you to type everything out. You can control the extent of PLUTO's interaction and customize the level of difficulty, from easy to hard. You can control the amount of words and how many sentences.

## Features
- 🐕 Always available from the side of your screen
- 🤖 Real AI answers powered by Groq (free!)
- 🎤 Voice input — talk to Pluto
- 🔊 Pluto talks back with Auto-speak
- 🖥️ My Screen — Pluto looks at your screen and helps
- 📎 Upload images and files

## How to use
1. Download the app for Mac or Windows from Releases
2. Open Pluto
3. Go to console.groq.com and get a free API key
4. Paste it in Pluto and you're good to go!
